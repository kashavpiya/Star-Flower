About Us

Star Flower Clinic in Fulton, Illinois has been dedicated to helping patients obtain medical cannabis for a variety of ailments since 2016. Our mission is to provide the best medical care when it comes to medical cannabis.

Taron Hutchings was born and raised in Galesburg, Illinois. He has used medical cannabis for many years and has seen how cannabis can work as an effective medicine. When he is not working in the office, he enjoys cooking for his friends and family.

Dr. Woods was born and raised in Iowa. His mother was a nurse who passed her caring and compassion way to her oldest son. He has lived in Morrison, Illinois for over 32 years and raised two sons there. His young granddaughters are the joys of his life, not to mention his lovely wife Liz. However his loyalty is truly to the Iowa Hawkeyes as he graduated from the University of Iowa.

Renee Applebey has lived in Illinois for almost 20 years and has worked in the cannabis industry since 2015. After her stepdad got sick with cancer, she started to realize that cannabis was not only a recreational drug, but also a medicine. After taking a trip to Colorado, Renee made it her passion to get people onto the medical cannabis program in Illinois and to further research how cannabis can be used for not only physical health conditions but also brain health too.
